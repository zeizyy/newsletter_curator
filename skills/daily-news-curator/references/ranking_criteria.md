# Ranking Criteria

Apply this order when selecting final stories:

1. Markets and macro impact.
2. AI platform and model developments with business impact.
3. Top global or U.S. news with likely market or tech implications.

Tie-breakers:

- Prefer primary reporting over commentary.
- Prefer fresher stories in the current window.
- Prefer stories with concrete numbers, policy changes, or strategic implications.
- Penalize duplicate wire rewrites and low-information headlines.
